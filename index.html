📷 Identify gear from a photo

We'll do our best to identify your gear from a photo. For the best results:

☀️
🎒
BRAND
  • 🧹Place the gear by itself on a clean surface
  • 🏷️Have any brand or model markings facing the camera
  • 💡Use good lighting — daylight works great
  • 📐Fill the frame, front-on — angled shots are harder to read
  • 🪪Got a hangtag or spec sticker? Even better — include it
  • 🧥For soft stuff (jackets, sleeping bags), lay it flat so the shape shows
